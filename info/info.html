About FFTabClose

Features

FFTabClose helps you keep your browser organized by automatically closing tabs you haven't used in a while.

How the Timer Works

Each tab has its own timer that starts when it's opened. Tabs are closed or discarded automatically once they exceed your specified time limit. Your settings persist even when restarting the browser.

Tab Exclusions

Tabs that are playing audio are never closed or discarded. Pinned tabs are not closed but can be unloaded to save memory.

Privacy & Permissions

FFTabClose requires the following permissions:

  • tabs - To monitor and manage tabs
  • storage - To save your preferences
  • alarms - To schedule periodic tab checks
