__MSG_infoTimerHeading__

FFTabClose tracks each tab's last active time. When a tab hasn't been used for longer than your selected time limit, it becomes eligible for closing.

The extension periodically checks for inactive tabs and closes or discards them based on your settings.

Timer information persists even if you restart your browser, ensuring consistent tab management.

__MSG_infoExclusionsHeading__

The following tabs are protected from automatic closing:

  • Active tab - The tab you're currently viewing is never closed
  • Pinned tabs - Can be discarded (unloaded) instead if you enable that option
  • Audio tabs - Tabs playing audio/video will be preserved if you enable that option
  • Essential tabs - Tabs detected as essential in certain browsers are protected

__MSG_infoDiscardHeading__

Tab discarding is a memory-saving feature that unloads a tab's content from memory while keeping the tab in your tab bar. When you click on a discarded tab, it reloads automatically.

This is especially useful for pinned tabs that you want to keep accessible but don't need loaded all the time.

__MSG_infoPrivacy__

FFTabClose operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect any browsing data
  • Send any information to remote servers
  • Track your browsing habits

The extension only requires the following permissions:

  • tabs - To monitor and manage tabs
  • storage - To save your preferences
  • alarms - To schedule periodic tab checks
